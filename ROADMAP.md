# ProtoLM Product Roadmap

This document outlines the high-level roadmap and future capabilities planned for the ProtoLM hardware operating layer.

---

## 📅 Near-Term
* **Expanded MCAD/ECAD Integrations:** Broader compatibility with mechanical and electrical CAD systems to synchronize design metadata directly with your workspace.
* **Contextual Workflows:** Intelligent, file-specific actions to run design checks, verify schematics, and export BOM parameters instantly.
* **Unified Compliance Gates:** Automated checks to verify release package completeness and design histories.

---

## 📅 Mid-Term
* **Local-First Workspace Synchronization:** Advanced synchronization capabilities for seamless offline collaboration and real-time metadata updates.
* **Integrated Sourcing Workspaces:** Collaborative quoting panels mapping bills of materials directly to supplier RFQ streams.
* **Closed-Loop Quality Tracking:** Linking supplier non-conformance metrics and corrective actions directly back to core system requirements.

---

## 📅 Long-Term
* **Programmable Design Abstractions:** Supporting code-based design abstractions for automated hardware verification loops.
* **Collaborative Simulation Orchestration:** Unified spaces enabling multi-disciplinary simulations and verification reviews to operate cooperatively.
